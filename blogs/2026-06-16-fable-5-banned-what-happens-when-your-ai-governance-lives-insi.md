Anthropic launched Fable 5 on June 9, 2026, and at 5:21 PM Eastern on June 12, seventy-two hours later, the US government ordered the model offline for every user worldwide. The post uses this to argue that AI governance built into the model layer fails and must instead live at the runtime.
