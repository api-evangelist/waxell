This article examines how AI agents processing employee personally identifiable information operate in a governance blind spot most organizations haven't addressed, citing broad context windows and multi-agent data propagation as structural vulnerabilities. It outlines GDPR, HIPAA, and CCPA requirements that demand pre-execution data filtering rather than post-incident detection.
