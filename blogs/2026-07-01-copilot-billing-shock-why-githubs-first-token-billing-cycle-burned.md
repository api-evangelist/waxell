GitHub's switch to token-based billing for Copilot in June 2026 caused monthly bills to jump from $29 to $750+ for developers using AI agents, since agentic sessions consume far more tokens than standard queries and costs accumulate before account-level limits trigger. The article argues real cost control requires enforcement built into the agent's execution layer.
